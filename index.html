Illidan Stormrage was the first Demon Hunter, the self-proclaimed Lord of Outland, ruling from the Black Temple, and the creator of the Illidari. Born a night elf, he eventually became, in the words of Maiev Shadowsong, "neither a demon nor a night elf, but something more." He was the twin brother of Malfurion Stormrage and was in love with Tyrande Whisperwind. Illidan was an incredibly gifted sorcerer, whose power became difficult to measure after his meteoric rise as a Demon Hunter and his absorption of the Skull of Gul'dan.
